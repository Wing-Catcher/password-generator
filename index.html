Why Strong Passwords?

Strong passwords are essential for protecting your online accounts from cyber threats. A weak password can be easily cracked by hackers using automated tools, exposing your personal and financial information. To safeguard your data, it's crucial to use a password that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters. By creating strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts, you significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access. This simple step is one of the most effective ways to keep your online presence secure. Source: National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
